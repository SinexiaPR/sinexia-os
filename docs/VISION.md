# Product Vision

## What Sinexia OS Is

Sinexia OS is an **AI-powered administrative platform** for Sinexia's client companies. It centralizes the documents, reports, and operational workflows that Sinexia manages on behalf of each client.

It is **not accounting software**. Sinexia OS does not replace QuickBooks, payroll systems, or ERP tools. It is the **portal and intelligence layer** that sits above those systems — where clients submit information, receive Sinexia's work product, and get answers about their data.

---

## Core Idea

> One place for clients to **send**, **receive**, and **understand** their administrative information — with SinexIA as the operational assistant.

Clients upload documents and access published reports. Sinexia's team processes documents, publishes insights, and manages status behind the scenes. SinexIA reads structured data from those documents and reports so clients can ask practical questions without digging through files.

---

## SinexIA's Role

**SinexIA** is the AI assistant embedded in the portal. Its purpose is operational, not financial compliance:

- Answer questions about uploaded documents and published reports
- Surface summaries, totals, and comparisons from structured profiles
- Reduce back-and-forth with Sinexia for routine inquiries

SinexIA is grounded in **extracted structured data** first. OpenAI is used selectively when deterministic answers are not sufficient — keeping responses accurate and cost-efficient.

---

## Who It Serves

| User | Experience |
|------|------------|
| **Client users** | Upload documents, view reports, ask SinexIA, receive notifications |
| **Sinexia admins** | Manage inbox across companies, publish reports, monitor processing |

Each client company is isolated. Clients see only their company's data.

---

## Long-Term Direction

Sinexia OS should evolve into the **default operational interface** between Sinexia and its clients:

1. **More specialized extractors** per client document type (payroll, AR aging, bank statements)
2. **Richer notifications and activity** so clients always know what happened and what is pending
3. **Deeper SinexIA capabilities** — trends, period comparisons, proactive summaries
4. **Client-specific workflows** without turning the platform into generic accounting software

The platform stays focused: **centralize, process, publish, assist**.
